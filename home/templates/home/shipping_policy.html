Shipping Policy

Welcome to SIPS! We are committed to ensuring your wine arrives safely, efficiently, and in perfect condition. Below, you’ll find all the details about our shipping policy, including delivery times, costs, and other key information.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship within the United Kingdom and select international locations. If your location is outside our shipping zones, please contact us at support@sipswine.co.uk to discuss potential options.

Shipping Rates

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): £5.99 or free on orders over £50.00.
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): £9.99.
  • Local Delivery: Free for addresses within 25 miles of Gateshead.
  • International Shipping: Rates vary based on destination and order size. Exact costs will be calculated at checkout.

Order Processing Times

Orders placed before 3 PM GMT on business days will be processed the same day. Orders placed after this time or on weekends/holidays will be processed the next business day.

Delivery Timeline

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days.
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days.
  • International Shipping: Delivery times vary depending on the destination, typically within 7-14 business days.

Shipping Restrictions

  • We only sell and ship to individuals aged 18 or older. Proof of age may be required upon delivery.
  • Due to local regulations, we are unable to ship to Northern Ireland and the Scottish Highlands.
  • We cannot deliver to P.O. boxes or military addresses.

Packaging

At SIPS, we prioritize sustainability and wine safety. All orders are packed using eco-friendly materials designed to protect your wine during transit.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to monitor your package’s progress.

Damages or Issues

We take great care in packing your order, but if your wine arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at support@sipswine.co.uk with your order number and photos of the damaged items. We’ll resolve the issue promptly by replacing the damaged product or issuing a refund.

Returns and Refunds

Due to the nature of our products, we do not accept returns on wine once it has been shipped. However, if there is an issue with your order, please reach out to us, and we’ll do our best to make it right.

Questions?

If you have any questions or special requests regarding shipping, don’t hesitate to reach out to our friendly customer service team at support@sipswine.co.uk. We're here to help!
