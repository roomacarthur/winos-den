FAQ

What is the delivery timeline for my order?

Orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days for standard shipping and 1-2 business days for express shipping. International delivery times vary.

Can I return or exchange my wine?

We do not accept returns on wine once it has been shipped. However, if your product arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours for assistance.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use this number to monitor your shipment’s progress.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to select international locations. Rates and delivery times vary by destination.

Is there a minimum age to purchase wine?

Yes, you must be 18 or older to purchase wine from SIPS. Proof of age may be required upon delivery.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team at support@sipswine.co.uk. We’re here to help!
